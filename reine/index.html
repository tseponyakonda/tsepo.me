Smart Care in the Air

Reine uses advanced sensors to monitor the health and safety of vulnerable persons 24/7 without wearables, or any input from the person being cared for.

Read more

Reine is a human centred advance automation technology, created to significantly improve the outcomes of all high care stakeholders.

Features

  • Fall Detection

    Reine detects falls, instantaneously sending an alert to family and carers Reine can help you connect to emergency services if needed.

  • Illness Detection

    Reine rapidly identifies changes in body temperature, detecting fever and possible illness. This will allow service users to self-isolate instantly.

  • Fire Detection

    Hotspot and heat detection is instant any emergency will trigger alerts to family members and carers. Reine can help you connect with emergency services if needed.

  • Notifications

    The device can be set up to send personalised notification to any person or provider that is important to you and your wellbeing.

  • Gesturing

    Our unique gesturing algorithms allow our participants to use gesturing and movement to communicate their needs in new ways.

  • Modern and clean UI

    Control Reine from any device with an internet connection using a modern and robust cloud based interface.

  • Complete Privacy
  • Instantaneous Alerts
  • 24/7 Vital Monitoring
  • Cloud based Infrastructure
